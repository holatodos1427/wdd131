iCamba

The site name is iCamba. It is based on my last name, Cambeiro, combined with i to reflect the Apple technology products. The name is personal, easy to remember, and clearly communicates what the business is about.

Optional domain: iCamba.com

Site Purpose

iCamba is a local Apple product viweport. The website provides information about the products available in apple, such as iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch. It also shows the services offered, like device setup and trade-ins. Visitors can find producst information.

Scenarios

  • Which MacBook is the best option for a college student with a limited budget?
  • News from recent Apple products
  • Websites to buy the new Apple products
