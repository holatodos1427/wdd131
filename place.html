About Peru

Peru is a country located in western South America. It is bordered by Ecuador, Colombia, Brazil, Bolivia, and Chile. Peru is known for its rich history, including the ancient Inca Empire, as well as its diverse geography which includes the Andes mountains, the Amazon rainforest, and a long Pacific coastline.

The capital city is Lima, and the country has a population of about 33 million people. Peru is also famous for Machu Picchu, which is one of the most visited tourist destinations in the world.
